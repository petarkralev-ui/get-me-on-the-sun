Flying In

We help make the visit productive

✈️

Direct flights make short buying trips realistic

From most large European cities, you can fly direct — no need to route through Madrid or another connecting city. Because flights run frequently year-round, we can help you plan a focused trip around viewings, NIE appointments, bank meetings, and local checks.

💶

Regular routes support the whole purchase

Ryanair, easyJet, and Jet2 all serve Tenerife well, making trips back and forth genuinely affordable. That matters during the purchase, but also later when you want to visit the property, meet the management team, or combine a holiday with practical owner tasks.

🛬

We plan around the right airport and area

The island has two airports. Tenerife South is closer to the main southern resort areas — where many non-resident buyers purchase — so we help match your arrival point, viewing schedule, and transport plan to the areas you are seriously considering.

On The Island

From landing to appointments

🚗

We keep the day practical

Public transport exists, but if you are viewing properties, meeting agents, or handling paperwork across different parts of the island, timing matters. We help plan the route, appointments, local support, and transport so the trip is about decisions, not wasted hours.
