The Regime

We help you understand what may apply

💼

ZEC can be powerful, but only in the right structure

Companies registered under the ZEC (Zona Especial Canaria) pay a reduced 4% Corporate Income Tax rate on qualifying activity, instead of Spain's general rate of around 25%. We do not treat this as a simple sales hook: we help you understand whether it is even relevant, then bring in qualified advice if your plans justify a closer look.

🌍

International buyers need joined-up thinking

Dividends, interest, capital gains, rental income, and ownership structure can interact with your home country's rules. Spain has double-taxation agreements with more than fifty countries, plus EU frameworks in some cases, so we help put the right advisor conversations in place before the structure is locked in.

Qualifying

What advisors will usually check

The ZEC is a genuine business regime, not an automatic perk of owning property. If it might fit your wider plan, we help coordinate the right conversations around points such as:

01

Company setup

Whether a newly created entity or branch with real management in the Canary Islands would be appropriate for your plans.

02

Investment level

Whether the required business investment thresholds are realistic and connected to a genuine activity, not just the property purchase itself.

03

Local employment

Whether job-creation requirements can be met and maintained in a practical, compliant way.

04

Permitted activity

Whether your business activity sits within what the ZEC actually authorizes, and whether property rental activity should be structured differently.

Important — this is why we coordinate advice

Buying a personal property in Tenerife does not, by itself, qualify you for ZEC benefits. The ZEC is designed for companies running a real economic activity — with genuine investment and job creation — not simply for holding a house.

Where it can become relevant is if you set up a company to run your property as an actual rental business, structured to meet the investment and employment thresholds above. That's a real strategy, but it's a deliberate business decision, not something that happens automatically when you sign at the notary.

Our role is to help you ask this early and connect the right professionals around your specific circumstances — the requirements and thresholds above are a general guide, not a substitute for professional tax or legal advice.
